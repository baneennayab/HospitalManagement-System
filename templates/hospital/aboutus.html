Our Commitment to Healthcare Excellence

For over 25 years, Pakistan Medical Hospital has been at the forefront of providing exceptional medical care and advancing healthcare standards across Pakistan.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services to all communities in Pakistan through advanced medical technology, compassionate care, and continuous innovation in medical treatment and patient experience.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's leading healthcare institution, recognized for medical excellence, innovative research, and setting new standards in patient-centered care while training the next generation of medical professionals.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Pakistan Medical Hospital

Compassionate Care

Treating every patient with empathy, dignity, and respect, understanding that healing involves both medical treatment and emotional support.

Medical Excellence

Maintaining the highest standards of medical practice through continuous learning, advanced technology, and evidence-based treatment protocols.

Community Trust

Building lasting relationships with our communities through transparent communication, ethical practices, and accessible healthcare services.

Innovation

Embracing medical advancements and innovative approaches to improve patient outcomes and enhance healthcare delivery.

Team Collaboration

Fostering a culture of teamwork where specialists from different disciplines work together for comprehensive patient care.

Safety First

Ensuring the highest standards of patient safety, infection control, and quality assurance in all our facilities and procedures.

Our Journey in Healthcare

Milestones in our commitment to advancing healthcare in Pakistan

1998

Foundation Established

Pakistan Medical Hospital began as a 50-bed facility with a vision to provide quality healthcare to the local community in Karachi.

2005

Expansion to Multan

Opened our second facility in Multan, expanding our reach to serve the healthcare needs of Southern Punjab.

2012

Advanced Cardiac Center

Established a state-of-the-art cardiac care center with Pakistan's first digital catheterization lab.

2018

National Accreditation

Awarded national accreditation for excellence in healthcare services and patient safety standards.

2021

Telemedicine Launch

Pioneered telemedicine services across Pakistan, providing remote consultations to underserved areas.

2024

Medical Research Center

Opened Pakistan Medical Research Center focusing on local healthcare challenges and innovative solutions.
